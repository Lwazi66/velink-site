What is VeLink?

VeLink is a revolutionary mobile platform by Velemani Technologies that replaces traditional SIM cards. Make calls, send SMS, and use your number on any device — all over Wi-Fi or data. No physical SIM. No airtime. Just connect and go.

Features

  • SmartCall: Crystal-clear VoIP calls over Wi-Fi/data
  • SMS Messaging: Send & receive texts with your VeLink number
  • Wallet Top-Up: Fund your account using PayPal, Stripe, or Flutterwave
  • AI Inbox: Block spam, tag messages, and auto-reply intelligently
  • Cloud History: Messages sync across devices with offline retry
  • Virtual Numbers: Use for WhatsApp, Facebook, banking, and more

Why Choose VeLink?

  • No SIM card or airtime required
  • Works globally over any internet connection
  • Protects users from scams and spam using AI
  • Use your number across devices
  • Great for rural, remote, and underserved users
